Sacred Destinations

Complete Temple Darshan Circuit

Nashik holds immense spiritual significance — home to one of India's 12 Jyotirlingas and the site of the holy Kumbh Mela. Our tempo traveller covers every sacred site.

🛕
Jyotirlinga #10

Trimbakeshwar Temple

One of the 12 sacred Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, located 30 km from Nashik. The source of the Godavari river, Brahmagiri mountain, and the mystical three-faced Shivalinga make this the heart of every pilgrimage.

🌊
Ramayana Circuit

Panchavati

The legendary forest where Lord Ram, Sita, and Lakshmana spent their exile years. Visit Kalaram Mandir, Sita Gumpha cave, Tapovan, and the sacred Ram Kund where devotees take a holy dip in the Godavari.

⛰️
Shakti Peetha

Saptashrungi Temple

Perched 4,659 feet above sea level on the Saptashrungi hills, this ancient Shakti Peetha is dedicated to Goddess Saptashrungi Devi. The panoramic views and spiritual energy make it a truly elevated experience.

Your journey begins on the Mumbai–Nashik Expressway, sweeping past Thane and Bhiwandi before climbing the legendary Kasara Ghat — a serpentine forest pass with stunning Western Ghat views. As you descend into Igatpuri, the cool air of the Sahyadri mountains greets you. The final stretch brings you through lush valleys, vineyards, and welcoming Nashik — Maharashtra's spiritual heartland.
